                                                                                                                         MCCAIN CHEERS SUPREME COURT DECISION ON INTERNET FILTERS FOR SCHOOLS AND LIBRARIES June 23  2006 Washington  DC   Senator John McCain  Chairman of the Commerce  Science  and Transportation Committee today applauded the U S  Supreme Court s decision to uphold a law that requires public schools and libraries to put anti pornography Internet filters on their computers or lose federal funding    As a result of today s Supreme Court decision  parents can now feel secure that when they entrust their children to a public school or library there is some level of safety for their children when they go on line  McCain said   The Children s Internet Protection Act  S  97  was introduced by McCain in January of 1999 and co sponsored by Sen  Hollings  Ranking Member of the Commerce  Science  and Transportation Committee                                                    June 2006 Press Releases           Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Current record
