                                                                                                                         SENATOR McCAIN STATEMENT ON LITTORAL COMBAT SHIP PROGRAM April 13  2007 Washington  D C     U S  Senator John McCain  R AZ  today made the following statement about the U S  Navy Secretary   s decision on the Littoral Combat Ship program      Secretary Winter has decided to terminate the third ship on the Littoral Combat Ship  LCS  program after no agreement could be reached to reign in costs  While I look forward to receiving all of the details leading to Secretary Winter   s decision  as a lead advocate of acquisition reform  I have supported his actions to get this important shipbuilding program under control       I have lauded his decision to stop work  hold responsible acquisition officials accountable  and seek fixed price agreements  Against the backdrop of skyrocketing costs and unacceptable construction delays  the strong measure that he took today  appears necessary to help ensure that we can deliver these ships affordably to the fleet                                               April 2007 Press Releases           Browse by                                                                              Current record
