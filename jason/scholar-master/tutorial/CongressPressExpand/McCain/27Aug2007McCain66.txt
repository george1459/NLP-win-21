                                                                                                                         McCAIN STATEMENT ON PASSING OF FORMER CONGRESSWOMAN JENNIFER DUNN  September 5  2007 WASHINGTON  DC     U S  Senator John McCain  R AZ  today released the following statement about the passing of former Congresswoman Jennifer Dunn  R WA       Jennifer Dunn will be greatly missed  She was a dedicated and effective representative of the people of Washington State and played an important role in the Republican leadership in the U S  House of Representatives for many years  She will be remembered as a loyal and dedicated public servant who made a big difference in the lives of millions of parents and children through her advocacy of a national Amber Alert system  I appreciate her service and mourn her passing                                                September 2007 Press Releases           Browse by                                                              Current record
