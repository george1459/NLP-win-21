                                                                                                                         McCAIN STATEMENT ON THE NOMINATION OF JUDGE ALITO TO THE SUPREME COURT October 31  2005  U S  Senator John McCain  R AZ  released the following statement today regarding the nomination of Judge Samuel Alito to the Supreme Court of the United States  I am pleased that the President has nominated Judge Samuel Alito to serve on the Supreme Court  Judge Alitos record is one of a thoroughly experienced  capable  and principled jurist and lawyer who has dedicated his professional life to public service  I look forward to the confirmation hearings and I encourage my colleagues on both sides of the aisle to accord Judge Alito their serious and fair consideration                                                October 2005 Press Releases           Browse by                                                                                                                                              Current record
