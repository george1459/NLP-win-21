                                                                                                                         MCCAIN STATEMENT ON THE DEATH OF CORETTA SCOTT KING January 31  2006 Washington D C    U S  Senator John McCain  R AZ  today issued the following statement regarding the death of Coretta Scott King    Dr  Martin Luther King once said that our lives begin to end when we become silent about things that matter  Coretta Scott King  his wife and partner of many  many years passed away this week  but we cannot let her death silence the transformative power of her life and her family s accomplishments  America must endeavor to keep alive and vocal the vital legacy of equality and justice that she leaves behind  I trust that  in God s hands  she is today reunited in an eternal conversation with her husband about the things that mattered to them and the actions they took together to make our nation  our world   better place    end                                             January 2006 Press Releases           Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                              Current record
