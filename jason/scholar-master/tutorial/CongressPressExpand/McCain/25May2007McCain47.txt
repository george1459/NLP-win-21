                                                                                                                         SENATOR McCAIN COMMENDS COMMITTEE PASSAGE OF NATIONAL DEFENSE AUTHORIZATION FY  08 May 25  2007  WASHINGTON  DC     U S  Senator John McCain  R AZ  today praised the committee passage of the National Defense Authorization Bill Fiscal Year 08  The bill authorizes funding for the Department of Defense and the national security programs of the Department of Energy  and contains important earmark reform provisions and provisions that will reform defense acquisition    Maximizing the transparency of Members  requests is fundamental to good governance  As stewards of taxpayers  dollars  it is our duty to spend the people s money responsibly  and to do it in an open and honest fashion  I am pleased that the committee has accepted that responsibility  This year s bill proves that a strong national defense and saving taxpayers  dollars are  as it should be  bipartisan priorities  I thank Chairman Levin for his leadership and support  Senator McCain said   The bill authorizes  648 8 billion for national defense  which was included in the Presidents 2008 budget  The legislation provides a 3 5 percent across the board pay raise for all uniformed personnel and adds  4 billion to the President s budget for vehicles designed to protect U S  troops in Iraq and  Afghanistan      The legislation includes several management reforms    the establishment of a Chief Management Officer for the Department of Defense  regulation for armed contractors working in an area of combat operations  and tighter competition requirements for funds spent by the Department on contract services   I am pleased the committee has adopted meaningful defense acquisition reform and increased the transparency of Members  earmarks  These provisions substantially improve how the Pentagon buys its largest and most expensive weapons systems  ensure that taxpayers receive the best value for their money  and help guarantee that the  U S   maintains the strongest  most capable fighting force in the world                                                May 2007 Press Releases
