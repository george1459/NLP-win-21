                                                                                                                         SENATOR MCCAIN MOURNS THE PASSING OF RETIRED AIR FORCE COLONEL WARREN LILLY October 15  2007 Washington  D C   U S  Senator John McCain  R AZ  today released the following statement about the passing of his good friend and fellow prisoner of war  retired Air Force Colonel Warren  Bob  Lilly     Bob was not only a good friend  but a real American hero  Throughout the years we spent together as prisoners in Hanoi  I was inspired by his strength and loyal patriotism     The news of Bobs death was deeply saddening  and I mourn his loss along with his family and friends  He was like a brother  Bob will be greatly missed  but never forgotten                                                October 2007 Press Releases           Browse by                                                                                                              Current record
