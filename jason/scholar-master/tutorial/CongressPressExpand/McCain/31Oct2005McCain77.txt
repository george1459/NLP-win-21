                                                                                                                         SENATOR McCAIN STATEMENT ON THE PASSING OF ROSA PARKS October 31  2005  Rosa Parks was a civil rights icon who galvanized the American civil rights movement with her simple act of protest  She forced the national conscience to take a hard look at how racism and segregation tarnished American ideals  She compelled this nation to rethink its definitions of freedom and equality for all  Leaders like Rosa Parks come along once in a generation  she will be dearly missed but her legacy will never be forgotten  Our thoughts and prayers are with her family at this difficult time                                                   October 2005 Press Releases           Browse by                                                                                                                                                              Current record
