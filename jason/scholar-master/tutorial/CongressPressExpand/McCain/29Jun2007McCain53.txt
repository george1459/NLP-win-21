                                                                                                                         SENATOR McCAIN STATEMENT ON INDEPENDENCE DAY July 4  2007 WASHIGNTON  DC    U S  Senator John McCain  R AZ  today released the following statement about Independence Day 2007       As I celebrate the anniversary of our nation   s independence in Baghdad today  I am mindful of the value of our freedom  America is one of the freest and most prosperous countries in the world  offering a life of liberty and endless opportunity      On this Fourth of July  we must remember our men and women in uniform here in Iraq and around the world for the sacrifices they have made so that we may live in a free land    Our forefathers had a dream for our country  and we must continue to protect our nation  its people and our values  while we encourage the spread of freedom across the globe so that others may know the meaning of liberty                                                  July 2007 Press Releases           Browse by                                                              Current record
