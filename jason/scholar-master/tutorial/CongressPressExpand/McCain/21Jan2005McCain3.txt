                                                                                                                         McCAIN COMMENDS CHAIRMAN POWELL FOR HIS SERVICE January 21  2005  Today Senator McCain issued the following statement on the resignation of Federal Communications Commission Chairman Michael Powell  Chairman Michael Powell has spent the last eight years revolutionizing the telecommunications industry by championing new technologies and advocating competition  I commend him and thank him for his outstanding public service  Under his stewardship  the Commission s deregulatory decisions have increased investment in the telecommunications sector  allowing this segment of the economy to grow exponentially  As Chairman of the Senate Committee on Commerce  Science and Transportation during the majority of Powell s tenure  I enjoyed a close working relationship with Powell and will miss the intelligence and enthusiasm he brought to the chairmanship                                               January 2005 Press Releases           Browse by                                                                                                              Current record
