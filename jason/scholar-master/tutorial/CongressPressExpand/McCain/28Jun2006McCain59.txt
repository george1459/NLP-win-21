                                                                                                                         MCCAIN STATEMENT ON H R   5252  THE ADVANCED TELECOMMUNICATIONS AND OPPORTUNITY REFORM ACT June 28  2006 Washington  D C    Today  the Senate Committee on Commerce  Science  and Transportation approved by a vote of 15 7 to report the Advanced Telecommunications and Opportunity Reform Act   The following is a statement from Senator John McCain  R AZ    While I voted to send the legislation to the floor for consideration by the full Senate  I remain concerned that this bill does not do enough to help consumers  I will work to include greater deregulation and consumer protections when this bill is debated on the Senate floor and will oppose its passage if the bill is not further improved for the benefit of the American public   It is reported that over  30 million dollars has been spent by interested parties on advertising to promote various positions with respect to the Committee s legislation  I can only hope that consumers will see  30 million dollars  worth of savings should this bill be enacted   The bill included amendments sponsored by Senator McCain that would allow for the introduction of more Low Power FM radio stations and create a tax moratorium on wireless services                                                     June 2006 Press Releases           Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Current record
