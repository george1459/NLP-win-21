                                                                                                                         MCCAIN STATEMENT ON MAJORITY LEADER BILL FRIST November 29  2006  Washington D C    U S  Senator John McCain  R AZ  today expressed his gratitude for the leadership and dedication to public service exhibited by Senate Majority Leader Bill Frist    I commend Dr  Frist for his unyielding dedication and service to our country and the American public throughout his time in the Senate  We are lucky to have had Dr  Frist as a principled leader  a true humanitarian and a respected colleague who worked every day to advance the causes most important to our nation  His commitment to making America a safer place will be long remembered  said Sen  McCain    Dr  Frist has quietly led by example with his medical work in Africa   tirelessly working to improve the lives of those in poverty  I thank him for his leadership and friendship and I wish him continued success                                              November 2006 Press Releases           Browse by                                                                                                              Current record
