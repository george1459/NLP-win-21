                                                                                                                         SENATOR MCCAIN COMMENDS NORTHERN IRELAND FOR ESTABLISHING POWER SHARING AGREEMENT March 26  2007 Washington  D C      U S  Senator John McCain  R AZ  today issued the following statement regarding a power sharing deal reached by the leaders of Northern Ireland   s Protestant and Catholic parties      I offer my congratulations to those who have made possible today   s power sharing agreement in Northern Ireland  The term    historic    is an oft used clich    but today   s agreement between Rev  Ian Paisley and Mr  Gerry Adams may be just that       There is still a great deal to be accomplished between now and May 8  when the power sharing executive is to resume operations  but the weekend   s developments augur well  At long last  after so many decades of violence and pain  old enemies are seated at the same table  talking about a future of peace and prosperity  This  more than anything else  signifies that a new day for Northern Ireland may well have dawned      I thank and congratulate Rev  Paisley and Mr  Adams  as well as the governments of Great Britain and Ireland  both of which have toiled long to help reach this breakthrough  Above all  I congratulate the people of Northern Ireland for putting the past behind them and for embracing a future of cooperation  rather than conflict                                                 March 2007 Press Releases           Browse by                                                                                                                                              Current record
