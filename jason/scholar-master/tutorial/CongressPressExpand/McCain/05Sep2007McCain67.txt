                                                                                                                         McCAIN STATEMENT ON THE PASSING OF CONGRESSMAN GILLMOR September 5  2007 WASHINGTON  DC     U S  Senator John McCain  R AZ  today released the following statement about the death of Rep  Paul Gillmor  R OH     The U S  Congress and the people of Ohio have been well served by the leadership and dedication of Paul Gillmor  His time in the United States Air Force and his many years in both the U S  House of Representatives and the Ohio Senate are a testament to his dedication to public service  He cared deeply about his constituents in Ohio and he will be greatly missed                                               September 2007 Press Releases           Browse by                                                                              Current record
