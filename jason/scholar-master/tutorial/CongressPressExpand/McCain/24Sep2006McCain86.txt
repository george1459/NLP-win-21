                                                                                                                         SENATOR MCCAIN JOINS RELIGIOUS LEADERS TO REITERATE NEED FOR COMPREHENSIVE IMMIGRATION REFORM September 26  2006  Washington D C    U S  Senator John McCain  R AZ  today reiterated his firm commitment to comprehensive immigration reform and securing America s borders  McCain was joined by religious leaders and his colleagues Senators Ted Kennedy  D MA   Arlen Specter  R PA   Lindsey Graham  R SC  and Ken Salazar  D CO    I believe we must support any measures that are necessary to secure the borders and that provide an effective and comprehensive manner to manage the immigrants that currently work and live in the United States   Leaders and men of conscience have committed themselves to the betterment of our people  McCain said  It s imperative that we join together to provide some type of protections for these 12 million immigrants while we work to establish a guest worker program  which will take at least two years   Religious leaders were present to lend their support for the Senate legislation including  Dr  Richard Land  president of the Southern Baptist Ethics  Religious Liberty Commission  Rabbi David Saperstein  director of the Religious Action Center of Reform Judaism  Rev  Dr  Derrick Harkins  pastor of the historic Nineteenth Street Baptist Church  and Jaime Soto  the Bishop of Orange  California                                              September 2006 Press Releases           Browse by                                                                                                                                                                                                                                              Current record
