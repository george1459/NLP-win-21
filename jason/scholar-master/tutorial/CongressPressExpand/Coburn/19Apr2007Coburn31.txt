          Press Room                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      April 19  2007  WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Senator Tom Coburn  M D   R OK   issued the following statement regarding Attorney General Alberto Gonzales     Attorney General Gonzales is a good man and I have no doubt he is dedicated to serving our country  I do not believe the attorneys were dismissed to interfere with investigations or cases or because of their political positions  However  few would disagree this situation has been handled poorly  The reputations of the dismissed U S  attorneys have been damaged and the reputations of the Department of Justice and the attorney generals office have been tarnished  Dr  Coburn said   The attorney general sets the standard of leadership for the Department of Justice and the standard set by Mr  Gonzales in this situation has been unacceptably low  This situation was handled incompetently  It is generous to say the attorney generals communications about this matter have been inconsistent  The standards used to dismiss the U S  attorneys should be applied to the attorney general  Leadership requires accountability and mistakes should have consequences  In this instance  the consequence should be the resignation of the attorney general  Dr  Coburn said                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                        9 488 542 624 510 00      31 090 20 Per Citizen
