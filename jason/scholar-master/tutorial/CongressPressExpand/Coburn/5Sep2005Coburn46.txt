          Press Room                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     Honors Legacy of Late Chief Justice William Rehnquist   September 5  2005  WASHINGTON  D C       U S  Senator Tom Coburn  M D   a member of the Senate Judiciary Committee  released the following statement today       With the nomination of Judge John Roberts to the position of Chief Justice it is now more critical than ever for the Senate to move quickly  Judge Roberts deserves a fair hearing and a fair up or down on the floor of Senate before the Court reconvenes on October 1     Dr  Coburn said       The passing of Chief Justice William Rehnquist is a sad moment for our nation and a great loss to the Supreme Court  Justice Rehnquist was man of great character and intellect  His sense of duty and commitment to the country and Court he loved was an inspiration to our entire nation       In an era when many on the Court drifted with public opinion  Justice Rehnquist had the courage to affirm our constitutional moorings  He loved the rule of law but not necessarily the rule of judges  He will be profoundly missed     Dr  Coburn said                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     9 488 547 043 960 00      31 090 22 Per Citizen
