          Press Room                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      March 2  2005  WASHINGTON  D C       U S  Senator Tom Coburn  M D   a practicing physician  today joined U S  Representative Walter Jones  R N C   to express his strong support for    The Houses of Worship Free Speech Restoration Act     H R  235  The legislation authored by Jones would restore the First Amendment right of ministers to address moral and political issues  Under current law  a house of worship can lose its tax exempt status if a member of the clergy dares to speak out on these taboo topics       Our founders wrote the First Amendment to protect two vital freedoms  speech about political subjects and religious worship  Preserving our freedom requires us to defend the right of moral and spiritual leaders to speak out on issues of justice and morality without fear of coercion from their own government  In fact  throughout history     and most recently at the end of the Cold War     oppressive regimes fell when religious leaders dared to speak truth and defy tyranny     Dr  Coburn said       Those who want to silence religious leaders have turned the Constitution on its head     Dr  Coburn added     Today   s trend of constricting the rights of houses of worship while expanding the rights of so called entertainers was never envisioned by our founders  The First Amendment was written to protect our liberties  not grant a license to pioneers of indecency                                                                                                                                                                                                                                               9 488 546 324 510 00      31 090 21 Per Citizen
