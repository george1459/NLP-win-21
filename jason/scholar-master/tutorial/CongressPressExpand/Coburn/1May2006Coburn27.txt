          Press Room                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     New design more user friendly  features tools to highlight efforts to rein in federal spending May 1  2006  WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Senator Tom Coburn  M D   R OK  today announced the launch of his redesigned Web site    The new Senate office site offers taxpayers a user friendly  interactive tool to stay informed of Dr  Coburns efforts to rein in federal spending  The redesign of this site comes after the launch of a separate  brand new Web site    that features Dr  Coburns activities as chairman of the Senate Subcommittee on Federal Financial Management  Transparency  accountability and access to reliable information are critical in our system of government  Taxpayers want to hold their government accountable for the expenditure of their money  I hope my Web site will provide taxpayers a user friendly and interactive tool they can use to accomplish that goal  Dr  Coburn said  One new feature on the site is a section titled  Right Now  which serves as a clearinghouse for information about Dr  Coburns amendments and bills  As the title indicates  Right Now features up to date details about Dr  Coburns current floor activities  The site also features a taxpayer whistleblower link  where citizens can anonymously submit tips on wasteful government spending programs                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         9 488 545 262 480 00      31 090 21 Per Citizen
