          Press Room                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      June 4  2007     TULSA   U S  Senator Tom Coburn  M D   R OK  today released the following statement on Oklahoma Senate Bill 1  the Taxpayer Transparency Act  The bill  which would create a searchable Internet database to track state government spending  is modeled after federal legislation authored by Dr  Coburn     Oklahomans should be concerned by attempts to resist transparency and openness in how their tax dollars are spent  Senate Bill 1 will give Oklahomans a powerful tool to hold their state elected officials accountable to ensure their tax dollars are spent in the most efficient and effective ways  Dr  Coburn said  The only reason to oppose transparency and openness in government would be if you have something to hide  I hope the governor will support transparency and openness in government and quickly sign state Senate Bill 1 into law                                                                                                                                                                                                                            9 488 542 847 200 00      31 090 20 Per Citizen
