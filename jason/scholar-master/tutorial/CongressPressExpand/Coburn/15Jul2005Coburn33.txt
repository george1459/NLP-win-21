          Press Room                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      July 15  2005   WASHINGTON  D C       U S  Senator Tom Coburn  M D   R OK   a practicing physician and Chairman of the Senate Homeland Security and Governmental Affairs Subcommittee on Federal Financial Management  Government Information  and International Security  held a hearing today examining the role of the World Trade Organization and its impact on American national sovereignty and economic security        Congress has an obligation to protect American sovereignty when the WTO attempts to impose policies that contradict our national interests  In several cases  the WTO has usurped U S  laws and become a tool for international political activism  The American people and their elected representatives  not international diplomats and activists  should determine which U S  laws are acceptable and which are not     Dr  Coburn said  Dr  Coburn explained that since WTO inception ten years ago  the U S  has lost half of the cases brought against us by other WTO members     25 cases out of 50  Already  Congress has repealed two laws by WTO dictate  These include   1  Foreign Sales Corporation  FSC  provisions that provided tax benefit for U S  exporters and  2  Anti Dumping Act of 1916  Both laws were created to protect U S  financial interests and were modified by WTO fiat to accommodate the interests of foreign countries    Dr  Coburn added that unlike other international institutions in which the U S  participates  the WTO links its adjudication process to an enforcement mechanism  Using this mechanism  international diplomats determine if U S  laws and regulations are acceptable or unacceptable according to the political trade standards of the international community  If nations don   t change laws that WTO rules against  WTO can and does impose punitive damages on that nation   s taxpayers and trade sanctions       I believe in free trade  On balance  free and fair trade with every nation benefits America and the world     Dr  Coburn said     However  Congress must do more to force the WTO to act fairly  The WTO is not only going out of its way to contradict U S  law  they are failing to fulfill their mandate to protect the intellectual property rights of American companies  Truly free and fair trade can help protect our economic and national security  but Congress has to be careful that we don   t sacrifice our sovereignty in the process
