          Press Room                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      October 27  2005  WASHINGTON  D C       U S  Senator Tom Coburn  M D   R OK  released the following statement today regarding Harriet Miers    decision to withdraw her nomination to the U S  Supreme Court       Today   s decision brings to a close a difficult chapter for a woman of great character and accomplishment  Ms  Miers    decision to put the interests of the country ahead of her own desires is further testament to her personal integrity          I would urge the President to quickly choose a new nominee among the large pool of highly qualified candidates who are capable of being confirmed by the Senate  The American people should pray for our President as he faces this difficult decision          The selection of the next Supreme Court justice will affect the lives of every American citizen for decades  Yet  as critical as this decision is I would remind my friends and allies that the Supreme Court will not  by itself  renew American culture  Families  not judges  are the foundation of our society  The greatest political act we can do is to love our families and win the hearts and minds of our neighbors  Our actions and rhetoric should match this undeniable reality                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                             9 488 547 592 110 00      31 090 22 Per Citizen
