          Press Room                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      September 9  2005  WASHINGTON  D C       U S  Sen  Tom Coburn  M D   R OK  today introduced a resolution supporting the goals and ideals of Gold Star Mothers Day  The measure has 11 co sponsors    The American Gold Star Mothers is composed of mothers who have had a child die while in service to our country  It is a federally chartered organization and works closely with many veterans    organizations    Gold Star Mothers have made the ultimate sacrifice of motherhood by losing their child in service to our country  It is paramount we show our gratitude and support for them by honoring that sacrifice     Dr  Coburn said     Though words can never heal their wounds  we can let them know we will never forget their sacrifice or that of their children       The resolution also requests President Bush issue a proclamation calling on the American people to observe Gold Star Mothers Day  which will be celebrated Sept  25                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                     9 488 547 078 220 00      31 090 22 Per Citizen
