          Press Room                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      June 8  2007     WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Senator Tom Coburn  M D   R OK  today commented on the Senates failure to reach passage of the Senate immigration bill  S  1348      The failure of this amnesty bill is a victory for fairness  and the rule of law  Though there were good provisions in the bill  such as a merit based immigration system  the overall bill would have been harmful to our nation and would have done nothing to solve our problems with illegal immigration  It is my hope the Senate will work together to produce a bill that protects our nation by first securing our borders and enforcing our laws     Dr  Coburn offered an amendment to the immigration bill  Amendment No  1311  that required Congress to secure our borders and enforce existing immigration laws before any provisions in S  1348 could be implemented  The Senate rejected the Coburn amendment by a vote of 42 54                                                                                                                                                                                                                            9 488 542 881 460 00      31 090 20 Per Citizen
