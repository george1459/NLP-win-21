          Press Room                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      September 29  2006  WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Senator Tom Coburn  M D   R OK   released the following statement following the passage of S  3930  the Military Commissions Bill   This legislation will give the president the tools he needs to protect American lives without compromising our core democratic values  The terrorists weve captured are not common criminals or even Prisoners of War  but are unlawful enemy combatants whose agenda is to end innocent lives  This bill will ensure that we may continue to prosecute detainees for heinous crimes in military commissions  At the same time  our intelligence and law enforcement agencies can continue to humanely detain and interrogate terrorists in order to prevent attacks and save lives  I would add  however  that this legislation in no way validates the Supreme Courts deeply flawed Hamdan decision  Terrorist organizations like al Qaeda who do not think twice about beheading civilians or murdering captured troops and are not signatories of the Geneva Convention are not entitled to Geneva Convention protections under the law  Yet  the fact that we treat our detainees humanely while our enemies do not is a sign of our strength  and should reassure Americans that in this global war the principles of freedom and justice will prevail over tyranny                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      9 488 545 930 530 00      31 090 21 Per Citizen
