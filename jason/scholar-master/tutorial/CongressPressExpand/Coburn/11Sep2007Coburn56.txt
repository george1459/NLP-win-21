          Press Room                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      September 11  2007     WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Senator Tom Coburn  M D   R OK  today released the following statement concerning General David Petraeus report to Congress on the U S  military efforts in Iraq     I have full confidence in General David Petraeus ability to lead our forces in Iraq and I believe he has done a tremendous job considering the enormity of the challenges in front of him  As Americans  we owe him and every brave soldier a tremendous debt of gratitude for their service and sacrifice  Dr  Coburn said  As the general  along with Ambassador Ryan Crocker  provides his report on Iraq  I hope the Senate gives fair consideration to his testimony  Congress specifically requested this report be given and they should not simply dismiss its conclusions without hearing them in full  to do otherwise would be disrespectful of the generals hard work and the sacrifice of our troops implementing this new strategy     Most of all  regardless of what one believes about the war  Washington  D C   politicians should not brush aside his report and suggestions for Iraq because they do not fit into a predetermined view of the war  We will find the way forward in Iraq through honest discussion and frank assessment  not questioning the judgment or motives of those with whom we disagree  Dr  Coburn said                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                         9 488 543 138 400 00      31 090 21 Per Citizen
