          Press Room                                                                                                                                                                                                                                                      May 13  2005  WASHINGTON  D C       U S  Senator Tom Coburn  M D   R OK  today released the following statement regarding the 2005 Base Realignment and Closure Commission  BRAC  report      The tremendous gain Oklahoma stands to receive through this process is a tribute to the hard work of our military personnel and community leaders  Oklahoma has also benefited from the outstanding leadership of Senator Inhofe  As a member of the Armed Service Committee  Senator Inhofe has worked tirelessly to ensure that Pentagon and BRAC officials understand how vital Oklahoma bases are to our national security  I look forward to working with Senator Inhofe to ensure that these sound recommendations are implemented     Dr  Coburn said   According to the 2005 BRAC report  Fort Sill is expected to receive a very significant net gain of 3 444 military personnel and 161 civilian personnel  Tinker Air Force Base will receive an additional 355 civilian personnel  Tulsa International Airport Air Guard Station is expected to gain 103 military and civilian personnel while Vance Air Force Base will receive an additional 99 military and civilian personnel                                                                                                                                                                                                  9 488 546 530 070 00      31 090 21 Per Citizen
