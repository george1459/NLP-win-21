   Obama Statement on the Death of Dr  C  DeLores Tucker   U S  Senator Barack Obama                       Barack Obama                                                                           Obama Statement on the Death of Dr  C  DeLores Tucker Thursday  October 13  2005  FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  Obama Contact  Robert Gibbs or Tommy Vietor   202  228 5511  Illinois Contact  Julian Green     Date  October 13  2005 Obama Statement on the Death of Dr  C  DeLores Tucker   WASHINGTON   U S  Senator Barack Obama  D IL  today issued the following statement on the passing of Dr  C  DeLores Tucker     Dr  Tucker dedicated her life to fighting for civil rights  and she did it with grace  style and class   said Obama   She broke the mold by becoming the first African American to serve as secretary of the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania  and inspired women across the country through her outspoken leadership  She never stopped fighting for justice and equality for all  and those of us who believe in her cause won t soon forget her struggles and her triumphs  Today  my thoughts and prayers are with her family
