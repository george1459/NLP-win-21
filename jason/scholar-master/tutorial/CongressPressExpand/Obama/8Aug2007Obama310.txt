   Obama Statement on Rockford and Winnebago Floods   U S  Senator Barack Obama                       Barack Obama                                                                           Obama Statement on Rockford and Winnebago Floods Tuesday  August  7  2007  FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  CONTACT  Obama Press Office  202 228 5566 WASHINGTON  D C    U S  Senator Barack Obama  D IL  today issued the following statement on the flooding in Rockford and Winnebago County   As many Rockford and Winnebago County residents evacuate their homes and communities  my thoughts and prayers are with them as they begin the recovery process   I am committed to working with state and local officials and relief organizations to ensure our communities can quickly restore power  repair roads  and rebuild damaged homes after the flooding
