   Obama Commemorates Sacrifice of Tuskegee Airmen   U S  Senator Barack Obama                       Barack Obama                                                                           Obama Commemorates Sacrifice of Tuskegee Airmen Thursday  March 29  2007  FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  Contact  Ben LaBolt  March 29  2007 WASHINGTON  DC    U S  Senator Barack Obama  D IL  released the following statement on the Tuskegee Airmen receiving the Congressional Gold Medal     While today   s recognition was long overdue  it was a testament to the strength and courage of the Tuskegee Airmen  men who served and sacrificed for our nation without ever asking for anything in return   My career in public service was made possible by the path heroes like the Tuskegee Airmen trailblazed
