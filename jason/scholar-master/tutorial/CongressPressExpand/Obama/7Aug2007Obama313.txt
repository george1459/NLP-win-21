   Obama Statement on the Withdrawal of Equal Employment Opportunity Commission Nominee David Palmer   U S  Senator Barack Obama                       Barack Obama                                                                           Obama Statement on the Withdrawal of Equal Employment Opportunity Commission Nominee David Palmer Monday  August  6  2007  FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE   CONTACT  Obama Press Office  202 228 5566 WASHINGTON  D C      U S  Senator Barack Obama  D IL  today issued the following statement on the withdrawal of David Palmer to be a member of the Equal Employment Opportunity Commission    Given the serious concerns about Mr  Palmer s tenure at the Justice Department  his decision to withdraw his nomination was the right thing to do   Every Equal Employment Opportunity Commissioner must have a history of achievement and an unwavering commitment to enforcing anti discrimination laws in the workplace   The President should appoint a nominee with a clear record of fairness and the judgment necessary to enforce employment laws
