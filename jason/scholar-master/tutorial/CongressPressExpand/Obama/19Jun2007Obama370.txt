   Obama Announces More Than  3 Million for Rock Island Arsenal Fire and Police Station   U S  Senator Barack Obama                       Barack Obama                                                                           Obama Announces More Than  3 Million for Rock Island Arsenal Fire and Police Station Friday  June 15  2007  FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  Contact  Ben LaBolt WASHINGTON  D C      U S  Senator Barack Obama  D IL  today announced that the Senate Appropriations Committee approved his request for  3 35 million in federal funding to renovate and expand Rock Island Arsenal   s combined Fire and Police Station facility     which was originally constructed in 1874      Making sure our first responders have the facilities  resources  and equipment they need to do their jobs and keep us safe is a critical priority for the Quad Cities region     said Senator Obama      This important funding will modernize and expand the Rock Island Arsenal facility to allow police and fire personnel to effectively and rapidly respond to emergencies   We must continue to address the security needs of our country by strengthening the emergency preparedness of our local communities
