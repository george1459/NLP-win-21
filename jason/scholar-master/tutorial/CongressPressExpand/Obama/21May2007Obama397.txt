   Obama Statement on Anniversary of Landmark Brown v  Board of Education Decision   U S  Senator Barack Obama                       Barack Obama                                                                           Obama Statement on Anniversary of Landmark Brown v  Board of Education Decision Thursday  May 17  2007  WASHINGTON  D C        The Supreme Court   s landmark ruling in Brown v  Board of Education to desegregate our country   s public schools was not only a victory for African Americans  but a triumph for the soul of an entire nation   While this courageous decision opened doors for me and millions of my fellow Americans  many of today   s schools remain divided by resources and opportunities  and we must push forward until all children are afforded an equal chance to succeed
