   Obama Commends Senate for Setting Target Date to Redeploy Troops from Iraq   U S  Senator Barack Obama                       Barack Obama                                                                           Obama Commends Senate for Setting Target Date to Redeploy Troops from Iraq Tuesday  March 27  2007  FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE  CONTACT  Ben LaBolt  Supplemental contains same redeployment goal as Obama plan WASHINGTON  DC    U S  Senator Barack Obama today released the following statement on the Senate vote to preserve a goal of March 31  2008 for removing combat troops from Iraq   Obama is the sponsor of the Iraq De escalation Act  which sets the same goal for redeployment     The Senate today made clear today that the goal to redeploy our combat troops from Iraq should be March 31  2008  the same target date in the Iraq De escalation Act   No military solution to this war is possible     this additional funding must provide for the safe redeployment of our troops   It is time to begin redeploying our brave soldiers from Iraq  so they can turn their attention to other missions  and to pursue a process of political reconciliation that heals the region
