   Obama Statement on the Passing of Former Maryland Rep  Parren Mitchell   U S  Senator Barack Obama                       Barack Obama                                                                           Obama Statement on the Passing of Former Maryland Rep  Parren Mitchell Tuesday  May 29  2007  Founding Member of the CBC WASHINGTON  D C      Congressman Parren Mitchell was an American trailblazer   His fight for civil rights evolved into a lifetime commitment to increasing minority business ownership and and battling poverty in our communities  Congressman Mitchell helped the Congressional Black Caucus grow in size and influence each Congress  providing inspiration to the next generation of African American leaders  My thoughts and prayers are with the Mitchell family  It s men like Parren Mitchell that made my career in public service possible
