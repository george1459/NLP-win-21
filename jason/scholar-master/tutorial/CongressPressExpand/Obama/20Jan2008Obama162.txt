   Obama Statement on the Passing of Former Chicago Mayor Eugene Sawyer   U S  Senator Barack Obama                       Barack Obama                                                                          Obama Statement on the Passing of Former Chicago Mayor Eugene Sawyer Sunday  January 20  2008  FOR IMMEDIATE RELEASE              CONTACT  Michael Ortiz WASHINGTON  D C      U S  Senator Barack Obama  D IL  today released the following statement on the passing of former Chicago Mayor Eugene Sawyer    I was deeply saddened to learn of the death of Mayor Eugene Sawyer  whose dedicated public service and thoughtful leadership guided the City of Chicago through troubled and turbulent times   For sixteen years  Eugene fought for people at the local level as an alderman of the 6th ward  before answering his government s call to service to become Chicago s caretaker   Michelle and I send our thoughts and prayers to his family                                  Pursuant to  policy  newsletters  petitions  opinion polls  and issue alerts and other electronic communications cannot be initiated by this office for the 60 day period immediately before the date of a primary or general election
