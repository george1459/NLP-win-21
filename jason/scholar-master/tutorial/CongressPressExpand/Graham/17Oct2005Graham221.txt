                 Date  10 17 2005  Graham Announces  480 000 in Save America  s Treasures Grants                                                                                                                                                                                                      WASHINGTON  U S  Senator Lindsey Graham  R South Carolina  today announced the Old Exchange Commission in Charleston will receive a  250 000 grant and the Historic Beaufort Foundation will receive  230 000        The Old Exchange Commission will use the funds to support the repair of cracked and delaminated stucco and masonry at the Old Exchange and Provost   The Historic Beaufort Foundation will use the funds to repair damaged exterior stucco at the Beaufort Arsenal        The Save America  s Treasures grants were awarded by the U S  Department of Interior  s National Park Service                                                                    October 2005 Press Releases
