                 Date  09 20 2005  Graham Announces  216 000 Grant for Claflin University                                                                                                                                                                                                            WASHINGTON   U S  Senator Lindsey Graham  R South Carolina  today announced the Claflin University Counseling Center will receive a  216 913 grant to enhance the education environment for students with disabilities        The funds will be used to provide training  technical assistance  and services to faculty and administrators to provide them with the skills necessary to teach students with disabilities   In addition  funds will be used to assess current services  policies  and procedures for serving students with disabilities        The grant was awarded by the U S  Department of Education                                                                      September 2005
