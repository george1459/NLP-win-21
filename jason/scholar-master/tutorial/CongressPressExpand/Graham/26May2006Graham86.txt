                                                                               Senator Graham Press Relases Contact  Wes Hickman  202 224 5972  or Kevin Bishop  864 250 1417  Date  05 26 2006  Senate Confirms Brett Kavanaugh to Circuit Court                                                                                                                                                                                                                  WASHINGTON   U S  Senator Lindsey Graham  R South Carolina  today voted to confirm Brett Kavanaugh to be U S  Circuit Judge for the District of Columbia Circuit   Kavanaugh was confirmed by a vote of 57 36        After the vote Graham made the following statement          Brett Kavanaugh is an outstanding selection by President Bush to serve on the D C  Circuit Court   He served in the Bush administration  was an advocate before the Supreme Court for the Solicitor General  s office  and was chosen to be a Supreme Court clerk   He has excelled academically and professionally          Mr  Kavanaugh is a solid conservative with a deep understanding of our legal system   He also has real world legal experience that will serve our nation for years to come   I  m pleased the Senate has confirmed him to this important position at such a crucial time in American legal history
