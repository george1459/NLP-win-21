                 Date  10 05 2005  Graham Announces  1 5 Million for Infrastructure Improvements in New Ellenton                                                                                                                                                                                     WASHINGTON   U S  Senator Lindsey Graham  R South Carolina  today announced Talatha Rural Community Water District and the City of New Ellenton will receive a  1 517 511 grant for water and sewer infrastructure improvements        The funds will be used to construct a new well  a 600 000 gallon elevated water tank  4 000 linear feet of 12 inch water line  and 8 000 linear feet of gravity sewer line   The improvements will serve a manufacturing facility          Adequate infrastructure is a major factor in future economic development     said Graham     This is an investment in the community and will have an impact for years to come           The grant was awarded by the U S  Department of
