                                                                                                                                                 Press Releases                                                                                                                         Senator Graham Press Relases Contact  Wes Hickman  202 224 5972  or Kevin Bishop  864 250 1417  Date  06 01 2006  Graham Announces  4 Million in Federal Aid for Johnsonville                                                                                                                                                                                                       WASHINGTON   U S  Senator Lindsey Graham  R South Carolina  today announced Johnsonville will receive a  949 400 grant and a  3 092 000 loan for water system improvements        The funds will be used to install approximately 28 miles of water line  a well  a 300 000 gallon elevated storage tank  and a SCADA system   In addition  the funds will be used to replace galvanized water lines  valves  and meters        The loan and grant were awarded by the U S  Department of Agriculture                                                                    June 2006 Press Releases           Browse by                                               Current record
