                                                                               Senator Graham Press Relases Contact  Wes Hickman  202 224 5972  or Kevin Bishop  864 250 1417  Date  09 06 2006  Graham and DeMint Announce  400 000 for HIV AIDS Oral Health Initiative                                                                                                                                                                                           WASHINGTON   U S  Senators Lindsey Graham and Jim DeMint today announced Sandhills Medical Foundation in Jefferson will receive a  400 000 Ryan White CARE Act grant to implement an HIV AIDS oral health initiative        The funds will be used to provide oral health care to persons living with HIV or AIDS in underserved areas where oral health services are inadequate or do not exist   Sandhills Medical Foundation is one of fifteen sites selected nationwide to implement a demonstration project using novel approaches to provide care        The grant was awarded by the U S  Department of Health and Human Services                                                                    September 2006 Press Releases           Browse by
