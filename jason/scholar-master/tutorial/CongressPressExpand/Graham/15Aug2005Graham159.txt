                 Date  08 15 2005  Statement from U S  Senator Lindsey Graham                                                                                                                                                                                                                        WASHINGTON    U S  Senator Lindsey Graham  R South Carolina  made the following statement on the passing of former Congressman William Jennings Bryan Dorn        Graham said          Congressman Dorn never lost his desire for service and was always a friend to our nation  s veterans   He served his state and nation with great character and integrity   With his passing South Carolina has lost one of her most noble statesmen                                                                         August
