                 Date  06 08 2005  Graham Announces  288 000 for Black River Healthcare                                                                                                                                                                                                              WASHINGTON    U S  Senator Lindsey Graham  R South Carolina  today announced Black River Healthcare  Inc  will receive a  288 489 community health center grant to expand services in Williamsburg County        The grant will help fund three new medical providers  additional exam rooms  clinical support staff  and a management position         Black River Healthcare is headquartered in Manning and has additional clinics in Greeleyville  Kingstree  and Olanta         Community Health Center grants are designed to promote the development and operation of community based primary health care service systems in medically underserved areas         The grant was awarded by the U S  Department of Health and Human Services                                                                        June 2005 Press Releases           Browse by
