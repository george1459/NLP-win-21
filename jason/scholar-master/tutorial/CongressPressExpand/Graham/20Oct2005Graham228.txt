                 Date  10 20 2005  Graham Announces  1 9 Million for Midlands Tech receive a  1 964 563 grant to support the school  s health sciences programs   The Community Based Job Training Initiative grant will help match students with service organizations that will provide clinical experiences and career development opportunities        The grant was awarded by the U S  Department of Labor                                                                    October 2005
