15 2006  Statement on Vice President Cheney                                                                                                                                                                                                                                WASHINGTON    U S  Senator Lindsey Graham  R South Carolina  today made the following statement in response to a question about Vice President Cheney  s recent hunting trip         I have been hunting with Vice President Cheney on several occasions over the past few years and know him to be a skilled and safety conscious hunter         Unfortunately  hunting accidents occur  even among the most skilled hunters   The Vice President has accepted personal responsibility for this accident and I know this is something he deeply regrets         Mr  Whittington and the Vice President have been friends for many years and I know Mr  Whittington  s personal privacy is a great concern to the Vice President   The Vice President knows the more he talks  the less privacy Mr  Whittington and his family will enjoy           The constant second guessing of what the Vice President should have said and when he should have said it is an unnecessary diversion from the many issues facing our nation                                                                        February 2006 Press Releases           Browse by
