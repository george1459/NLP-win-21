                                                                               Senator Graham Press Relases Contact  Wes Hickman  202 224 5972  or Kevin Bishop  864 250 1417  Date  08 31 2006  Graham and DeMint Announce Nearly  1 Million for SLED to Help Reduce DNA Analysis Backlog                                                                                                                                                                         WASHINGTON  U S  Senators Lindsey Graham and Jim DeMint today announced the South Carolina Law Enforcement Division  SLED  will receive two grants for nearly  1 million to improve future capacity to conduct DNA tests and to help reduce the backlog of DNA analysis cases         SLED will receive a  666 200 grant to improve DNA testing infrastructure and analysis capacity at government owned labs to prevent future DNA backlogs   In addition  SLED will receive a  332 766 grant to analyze backlogged forensic DNA casework in government owned labs and private vendors        Both grants were awarded by the U S  Department of Justice                                                                     August 2006 Press Releases           Browse by
