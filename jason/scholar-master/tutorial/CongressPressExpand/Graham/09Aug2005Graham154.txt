                 Date  08 09 2005  Graham Announces  742 000 for Lancaster County School District                                                                                                                                                                                                    WASHINGTON   U S  Senator Lindsey Graham  R South Carolina  today announced Lancaster County School District will receive two federal grants for more than  742 000        They will receive a  492 423 grant to support the Lancaster County GEAR UP program       GEAR UP is a national grant program to support early college preparation and awareness activities for low income students   Lancaster County School District will provide a comprehensive network of services including tutoring  college awareness workshops  enhanced guidance  parent workshops  field trips to colleges and universities  and a scholarship program funded by a private foundation        The school district will also receive a  250 441 grant to provide professional development for arts educators   The funds will be used to support professional development programs in elementary and secondary schools for music  dance  drama  media arts  visual arts  and other arts programs        Both grants were awarded by the U S  Department of Education                                                                      August 2005
