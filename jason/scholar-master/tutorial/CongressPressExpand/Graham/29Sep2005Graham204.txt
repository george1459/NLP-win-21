                 Date  09 29 2005  Graham Announces  1 3 Million for Bioterrorism Training                                                                                                                                                                                                           WASHINGTON   U S  Senator Lindsey Graham  R South Carolina  today announced the Medical University of South Carolina  MUSC  will receive a  1 331 869 grant for bioterrorism training        The funds will be used to train health care professionals to recognize and respond to bioterrorism and implement a notification communication system   MUSC partners with the University of South Carolina  South Carolina Department of Health and Environmental Control  South Carolina Center for Public Health Preparedness  South Carolina Hospital Association  South Carolina Office of Rural Health  South Carolina Primary Health Care Association  and others        Over the past two years  more than 12 000 health care professionals have received bioterrorism training including physicians  nurses  dentists  emergency medical personnel  pharmacists  and social workers        The grant was awarded by the U S  Department of Health and Human Services                                                                      September 2005 Press Releases           Browse by
