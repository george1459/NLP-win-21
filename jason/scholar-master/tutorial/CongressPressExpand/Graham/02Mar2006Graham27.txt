Passes PATRIOT Act Reauthorization                                                                                                                                                                                                                         WASHINGTON    U S  Senator Lindsey Graham  R South Carolina  today praised Senate passage of the PATRIOT Act reauthorization   The vote in the Senate was 89 10          The PATRIOT Act helps our intelligence and law enforcement agencies protect us from a vicious enemy   The tools it provides are necessary to help fight and win the War on Terror          The legislation also strikes a good balance protecting the rights of individuals while protecting the security of our nation   I  m pleased the Senate passed the extension and look forward to President Bush signing it into law                                                                        March 2006 Press Releases           Browse by
