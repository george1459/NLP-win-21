                                                                               Senator Graham Press Relases Contact  Wes Hickman  202 224 5972  or Kevin Bishop  864 250 1417  Date  06 06 2007  Graham  Brown  Wilson Announce  2 3 Million for Coastal Management in South Carolina  WASHINGTON  U S  Senator Lindsey Graham and U S  Representatives Henry Brown and Joe Wilson today announced the South Carolina Department of Health and Environmental Control will receive a  2 316 000 grant to help administer its coastal zone management  CZM  program  The funds will provide support for CZM planning  permitting  enforcement  outreach  and technical assistance  Additionally  funds may be used to support initiatives to create an ocean management plan and to build coastal community resilience to shoreline change  The grant was awarded by the U S  Department of Commerces National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration  NOAA   This award marks the 30th year that NOAA has provided coastal management assistance to South Carolina                                                            June 2007 Press Releases           Browse by
