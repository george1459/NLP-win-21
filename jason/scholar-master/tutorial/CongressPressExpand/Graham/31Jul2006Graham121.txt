                                                                               Senator Graham Press Relases Contact  Wes Hickman  202 224 5972  or Kevin Bishop  864 250 1417  Date  07 31 2006  Graham and DeMint Announce  1 7 Million for Branchville Water System                                                                                                                                                                                              WASHINGTON  U S  Senators Lindsey Graham and Jim DeMint today announced the town of Branchville will receive a  1 1 million grant and a  600 000 loan for water system improvements   The funds will be used to install water distribution lines from Branchville on Highway 21 to the Cattle Creek Road area of Orangeburg County  The project includes approximately 81 500 feet of water line  54 hydrants  and a 250 000 gallon elevated storage tank  There is currently no public water available in the project area   The grant and loan were awarded by the U S  Department of Agriculture                                                              July 2006 Press Releases           Browse by
