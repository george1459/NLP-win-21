                 Date  08 02 2005  Graham Announces Nearly  400 000 for Spartanburg to Fight Domestic Violence                                                                                                                                                                                       WASHINGTON   U S  Senator Lindsey Graham  R South Carolina  today announced the City of Spartanburg will receive a  397 416 grant to combat domestic violence        The grant will support Spartanburg  s collaborative effort with Safe Homes Rape Crisis to implement a multi disciplinary response to investigate and prosecute domestic violence cases        The grant was awarded by the U S  Department of Justice                                                                      August 2005 Press
