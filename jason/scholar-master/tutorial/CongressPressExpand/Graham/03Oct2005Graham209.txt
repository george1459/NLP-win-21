                 Date  10 03 2005  Graham Announces More Than  2 2 Million for USC Health Care and Research Programs                                                                                                                                                                                 WASHINGTON   U S  Senator Lindsey Graham  R South Carolina  today announced the University of South Carolina  USC  will receive more than  2 2 million dollars in grants to support health care and medical research programs        Grants awarded include        1 585 298 to support efforts to eliminate disparities in health care           329 871 for drug abuse research           295 387 for genomics and biomedical research       All grants were awarded by the U S  Department of Health and Human
