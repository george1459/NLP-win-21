                 Date  07 05 2005  Graham Announces  200 000 for Beech Island Historical Society                                                                                                                                                                                                     WASHINGTON    U S  Senator Lindsey Graham today announced the Beech Island Historical Society will receive a  200 000 grant to rehabilitate a turn of the century brick cottonseed barn for use as an agricultural museum   The museum is a recognized site on the Region III South Carolina National Heritage Corridor for Aiken County        The grant was awarded by the U S  Department of Agriculture                                                                    July 2005 Press Releases
