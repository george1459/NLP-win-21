                 Date  08 08 2005  Graham Announces  584 000 in Federal Aid for Calhoun Falls                                                                                                                                                                                                        WASHINGTON   U S  Senator Lindsey Graham  R South Carolina  today announced Calhoun Falls will receive a  373 100 grant and a  211 000 loan for infrastructure relocation        The funds will be used to relocate water and sewer lines along the new rights of way of Highway 72   The South Carolina Department of Transportation will also contribute  334 226 to this project        The grant and loan were awarded by the U S  Department of Agriculture
