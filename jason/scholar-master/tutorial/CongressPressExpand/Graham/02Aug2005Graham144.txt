                 Date  08 02 2005  Graham Announces  2 65 Million for Anderson Regional Airport                                                                                                                                                                                                      WASHINGTON   U S  Senator Lindsey Graham  R South Carolina  today announced the Anderson Regional Airport will receive a  2 65 million grant for airport improvements        The funds will be used for the final phase of a runway and taxiway extension  and to relocate the medium intensity approach lighting system        The grant was awarded by the U S  Department of Transportation                                                                      August 2005 Press Releases           Browse by
