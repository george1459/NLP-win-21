                 Date  04 27 2005  Graham Announces  950 000 for Marion Water Company                                                                                                                                                                                                                WASHINGTON   U S  Senator Lindsey Graham  R South Carolina  today announced Marco Rural Water Company in Marion will receive a  259 300 grant and  693 000 loan for water line relocation        The funds will be used to relocate 33 722 feet of existing water line along US Highway 501   The relocation is necessary due to South Carolina Department of Transportation construction        The funds were awarded by the U S  Department of Agriculture
