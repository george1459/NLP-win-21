                 Date  08 23 2005  Graham Announces  894 000 for Lancaster County School District                                                                                                                                                                                                    WASHINGTON   U S  Senator Lindsey Graham  R South Carolina  today announced the Lancaster County School District will receive an  894 523 Safe Schools Healthy Students grant        The Safe Schools Healthy Students Initiative is a national grant program that provides federal funds to create safe and drug free schools  develop social skills  and promote healthy childhood development        Graham recently visited Lancaster and met with local officials to discuss education  social security  and other issues of local concern        The grant was awarded by the U S  Department of Education                                                                      August 2005 Press Releases
