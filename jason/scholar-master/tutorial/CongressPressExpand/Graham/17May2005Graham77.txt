                 Date  05 17 2005  Statement on Currency Report to Congress                                                                                                                                                                                                                          WASHINGTON    U S  Senator Lindsey Graham  R South Carolina  today made the following statement on the U S  Treasury report on the currency practices of our trading partners   The report said the fixed exchange rate China maintains   is a substantial distortion to world markets    but did not find them to be manipulating their currency        Graham said          I hope the Bush Administration builds on this report and lets the Chinese know their currency must change or they will face severe consequences            For the first time  the Administration has called what China is doing a   distortion     They are moving in the right direction and I applaud them for beginning to take a tougher line in dealing with the Chinese   But make no mistake  more must be done          It  s pretty simple    China cheats   The Chinese manipulate their currency to gain an unfair advantage over the U S  and other nations   The chorus of voices opposing what China is doing is expanding as the G 7 and European Union both accuse China of manipulating its currency            We need to address this issue as soon as possible   It  s one thing if someone outworks or outsmarts us   But it  s not fair when someone is rigging the rules of the game to give them an unfair competitive advantage
