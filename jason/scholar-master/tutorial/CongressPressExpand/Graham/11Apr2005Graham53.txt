                 Date  04 11 2005  Graham Announces More Than  1 25 Million for Community Health Centers in  and Manning for community health center grants in  and Manning         New Horizon  s Family Health Services in  will receive a  522 938 grant  and Black River Healthcare in Manning will receive a  733 333 grant        Community Health Center grants are designed to promote the development and operation of community based primary health care service systems in medically underserved areas        The grant was
