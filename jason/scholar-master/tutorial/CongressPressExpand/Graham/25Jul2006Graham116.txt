                                                                               Senator Graham Press Relases Contact  Wes Hickman  202 224 5972  or Kevin Bishop  864 250 1417  Date  07 25 2006  Graham and DeMint Announce  500 000 for Character Education Programs in South Carolina Schools                                                                                                                                                                    WASHINGTON   U S  Senators Lindsey Graham and Jim DeMint today announced the South Carolina Department of Education will receive a  504 898 Partnerships in Character Education Grant        Funds will be used to design and implement character education programs that teach character elements such as caring  civic virtue  citizenship  justice  fairness  respect  responsibility  trustworthiness  and charity   In addition  programs seek to improve student attendance and academic performance  parental involvement  teacher development  and community involvement        The grant was awarded by the U S  Department of Education                                                                    July 2006 Press Releases           Browse
