                 Date  01 04 2006  Graham Announces  204 000 Grant for Charleston Health Center                                                                                                                                                                                                      WASHINGTON   U S  Senator Lindsey Graham  R South Carolina  today announced Charleston Interfaith Crisis Ministry will receive a  204 539 Healthcare for the Homeless  HCH  grant        The funds will help provide primary health care  mental health  and substance abuse services to the homeless population in the Charleston area   Interfaith Crisis Ministry has been a recipient of this competitive federal grant since 1996        The grant was awarded by the U S  Department of Health and Human Services
