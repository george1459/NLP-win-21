                 Date  01 04 2005  Graham Statement on Senator Jim DeMint                                                                                                                                                                                                                            WASHINGTON    U S  Senator Lindsey Graham  R South Carolina  today made the following statement on the swearing in of fellow U S  Senator Jim DeMint  R South Carolina           Jim is a great addition to the Senate   I think we  ll be good partners working together to move the conservative reform movement forward   He comes with years of experience in the House of Representatives and will hit the ground running           The committees Jim will serve on  particularly the Commerce Committee and the Public Works Committee  will be of great benefit to South Carolina  Between the two of us  our committees will cover a wide variety of issues and we  ll be helpful to our state          Jim  s a solid conservative who is open to new ideas about reforming the federal government   I really look forward to serving with him   He  s a first class guy and a great public servant
