                 Date  08 23 2005  Graham Announces  813 000 for Marion County School District Two                                                                                                                                                                                                   WASHINGTON   U S  Senator Lindsey Graham  R South Carolina  today announced Marion County School District Two will receive an  813 679 Safe Schools Healthy Students grant        The Safe Schools Healthy Students Initiative is a national grant program that provides federal funds to create safe and drug free schools  develop social skills  and promote healthy childhood development        The grant was awarded by the U S  Department of Education
