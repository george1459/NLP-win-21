    Date  01 31 2006  Statement on the Passing of Coretta Scott King                                                                                                                                                                                                                    WASHINGTON    U S  Senator Lindsey Graham  R South Carolina  today made the following statement on the death of Coretta Scott King          All Americans should celebrate a life well lived            Mrs  King was a strong voice for economic and social justice   She was a pioneer of the civil rights movement who stood on her own as one of the most effective voices in America expressing equality for all   She was a brave woman who held firm to her beliefs during some of America  s most difficult days   Her courage in standing for the idea of justice for all will be an inspiration for future generations of Americans            Mrs  King has rightfully earned her place in history as a great American
