                 Date  09 13 2005  Graham Announces  490 000 for Calhoun Falls                                                                                                                                                                                                                       WASHINGTON   U S  Senator Lindsey Graham  R South Carolina  today announced Calhoun Falls will receive a  214 400 grant and  276 000 loan for improvements in the water and sewer department        The funds will be used to transform an existing building and convert it into a maintenance facility   Calhoun Falls was previously awarded a  373 100 grant and  211 000 loan for this project        The grant and loan were awarded by the U S  Department of Agriculture
