                 Date  08 30 2005  Graham Announces  150 000 for SC State                                                                                                                                                                                                                            WASHINGTON   U S  Senator Lindsey Graham  R South Carolina  today announced South Carolina State University will receive a  150 000 grant to support the school  s rehabilitation counseling program        South Carolina State University  s rehabilitation counseling program was started in 1972 and was the first such program at a Historically Black College and University        The grant was awarded by the U S  Department of Education
