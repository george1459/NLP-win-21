                                                                               Senator Graham Press Relases Contact  Wes Hickman  202 224 5972  or Kevin Bishop  864 250 1417  Date  06 11 2007  Graham Announces  1 1 Million in Scientific Research Grants for Furman and  Tech  WASHINGTON  U S  Senator Lindsey Graham  R South Carolina  today announced Furman University and  Technical College will receive  1 1 million in scientific research grants  Furman Furman University will receive a  272 000 grant for a project entitled RUI  Photochemistry and Photophysics of Macrocyclic Chromium III  Complexes  An Investigation of Electronic Energy Self Exchange   Tech  Technical College will receive an  886 886 grant for a project entitled Virtual Simulated Inspection  ViSIns  Laboratory  Using Interactive 3D Knowledge Objects to Promote Learning for Non Destructive Inspection in Aviation Maintenance Technology  The grants were awarded by the National Science Foundation
