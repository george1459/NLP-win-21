                 Date  03 25 2005  Graham Announces  1 Million for Marine Corps Air Station Beaufort                                                                                                                                                                                                 WASHINGTON   U S  Senator Lindsey Graham  R South Carolina  today announced the Marine Corps Air Station Beaufort and Beaufort County will receive  1 million in federal funds to enter into an encroachment partnership        The funds will allow the partnership to purchase restrictive easements from willing owners of agricultural land near the Station to ensure future development does not encroach on military property or training activities          This is a unique partnership that demonstrates how a military installation and the community can work together for mutual benefit     said Graham     These funds will help preserve open space in the community  and address an issue important to the Air Station  encroachment           The funding comes from the U S  Marine Corps                                                                      March 2005 Press Releases           Browse by
