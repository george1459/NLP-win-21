                                                                                                                                                 Press Releases                                                                                                                         Senator Graham Press Relases Contact  Wes Hickman  202 224 5972  or Kevin Bishop  864 250 1417  Date  05 02 2007  Graham and DeMint Announce  5 4 Million in Federal Funds for Chesterfield County Water Project  WASHINGTON  U S  Senators Lindsey Graham and Jim DeMint today announced Chesterfield County Rural Water Company will receive a  1 369 500 grant and a  4 071 000 loan for water system infrastructure improvements   The funds will be used for the installation of 168 000 feet of waterline  the construction of a 500 000 gallon elevated water tank and booster pump station  a generator  and associated hydrants and controls   The grant and loan were awarded by the U S  Department of Agriculture as part of the agency s merit based or competitive allocation process                                                            May 2007 Press Releases           Browse by                                                                                                               Current record
