                 Date  04 07 2005  Graham Announces Nearly  1 4 Million for Conway Health Center                                                                                                                                                                                                     WASHINGTON    U S  Senator Lindsey Graham  R South Carolina  today announced Health Care Partners of South Carolina  Inc  in Conway will receive a  1 396 718 Community Health Center grant         Community Health Center grants are designed to promote the development and operation of community based primary health care service systems in medically underserved areas         The grant was awarded by the U S  Department of Health and Human Services
